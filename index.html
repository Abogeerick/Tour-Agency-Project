Popular Tours

Watamu

Escape to the coastal paradise of Watamu, where pristine white sandy beaches meet vibrant coral reefs. Immerse yourself in the crystal-clear turquoise waters, perfect for snorkeling and diving adventures. Discover a world of marine marvels as you swim alongside colorful fish and encounter gentle sea turtles, creating unforgettable memories in this tropical haven.

Maasai Mara

Embark on the ultimate African safari adventure in Maasai Mara, where the vast savannahs come alive with iconic wildlife. Witness the awe-inspiring annual migration of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles as they traverse the plains in search of fresh grazing lands.

Mt. Kenya

Discover the awe-inspiring beauty of Mt. Kenya, the highest mountain in Kenya and the second-highest in Africa. Immerse yourself in the breathtaking alpine landscapes, dotted with glacial lakes, dense forests, and towering peaks.
